LIT (Ladies in Tech)

About

GIT (Girls in Tech) Conference is a full day event centered on empowering girls to learn to code. So often, girls are just not exposed to the same level of coding and technology that their male peers are, so this conference is dedicated to encouraging girls to go out there and learn something new. Furthermore, the goal of this conference is to provide existing role models for young girls to explore a future in technology as well as for girls with programming experience already to meet like-minded peers.
